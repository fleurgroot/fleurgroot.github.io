about

I’m Fleur and I’m an illustrator and designer from New Zealand. I love illustrating fonts, characters, and pattern and my work almost always has humour or a cute clumsiness. I enjoy doing illustration and design as a means to create awareness or educate. In 2011, I started a label called sitdoggsit, where I design and illustrate eyecatching postcards and prints. Commissions are very very welcome!
